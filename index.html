About Kalvium:-

Kalvium is an Edu-tech company, On a quest to transform the higher education space in India by offering India’s 1st Liberal Engineering program in Computer Science with a work-integrated learning curriculum. With a mission to help young minds transform into thriving future tech leaders, Kalvium offers its 4-years on-campus program in collaboration with top accredited Universities in the country. The program is powered by Kalvium’s proprietary AI-powered technology, “Quadrangle”, which offers a unique and immersive learning experience moving away from the traditional chalk and talks learning approaches. The program curated by top CXOs/CTOs of the Tech-World aims to deliver students with real-world readiness and teach the latest technologies & skills that can make one a Full-Stack Developer even before graduation. Kalvium is trusted and backed by India's top technology leaders including Anupam Mittal (Founder, People Group), Sujeet Kumar (Co-founder at Udaan), Kunal Shah (Founder at CRED), Nithin Kamath (CEO at Zerodha), Rahul Chari (CTO at PhonePe), and many more. The founding team is a bunch of non-practicing engineers, inspired to make a difference in the education space and are experts in the field having a track record of training and placing over 50 Lakh students in more than 500+ industries in the past 13 years under their Coimbatore-based Startups FACE Prep & ProGrad. The program is Live with the 2022 batch of students taking up the program at Lovely Professional University, Punjab, and Coimbatore, Tamilnadu.
